Cookie Cowboy Bars Recipe

Description

Cookie Cowboy Bars are a chewy, golden treat that perfectly blend the best parts of a cookie and a bar. This old-fashioned recipe starts with a buttery brown sugar base mixed with oats, chocolate chips, shredded coconut, and chopped nuts for a hearty texture and rich flavor. A touch of vanilla and a sprinkle of salt balance the sweetness, while the chewy layers bake up golden on the edges and soft in the center. These bars are easy to make, deliciously nostalgic, and perfect for sharing at potlucks or family gatherings.

Once baked, let the bars cool slightly before cutting them into squares or rectangles. Each bite is packed with gooey chocolate, crunchy oats, and a hint of caramel-like flavor from the brown sugar. Cookie Cowboy Bars store well and taste even better the next day, making them a perfect make-ahead dessert. Enjoy them with a glass of cold milk or a warm cup of coffee for a sweet, satisfying treat thatâ€™s as comforting as it is crowd-pleasing.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line an 8x8-inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. Mix together flour, salt, cinnamon, and baking powder in a large bowl; set aside.
  3. Whisk together brown sugar, melted butter, eggs, and vanilla in a medium bowl. Add to flour mixture and stir until moistened
  4. Add oats, coconut, chocolate chips, and pecans to the dough. Fold with a spatula until well combined. (The mixture will be thick.)
  5. Transfer dough to the prepared baking pan and spread out evenly. Bake until the bars are golden brown and set on the edges, 25 to 30 minutes
  6. Transfer pan to a wire rack and cool in the pan. Cut into 12 portions.
